Understanding Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) and its prevention.

What is Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)?

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) is a condition characterized by the gradual loss of kidney function over time. CKD can lead to kidney failure, heart disease, or even stroke.

Common Symptoms of CKD:

  • Fatigue
  • Swelling in legs, ankles, and feet
  • Shortness of breath
  • Frequent urination

Prevention & Treatment:

Maintaining a healthy lifestyle, proper hydration, and regular check-ups can help prevent CKD.
